Sensors Used

The upgraded kick pad now features cutting-edge sensors, including force detectors integrated with an Arduino microcontroller, providing precise real-time data on each strike. These sensors accurately measure the force exerted, allowing athletes to monitor and adjust their performance with scientific precision. The Arduino microcontroller processes this data instantly, offering immediate feedback and ensuring that training is both effective and engaging. This combination of advanced sensor technology and microcontroller integration marks a significant leap forward in training equipment, helping athletes push their boundaries and achieve new levels of excellence.
